Little Challenger
(1 Vs 1)


An embeded multiplayer turn-based WebGL game. For server-side, I used Socket.IO in NodeJs, and for client-side, modified and used this Unity-Socket.IO plugin in Unity WebGL.
The gameplay is really simple and all you need to do is by pressing the die button within its parameter to get the exact die number in given seconds, and shoot your opponent with your full charged gun.
The following video is the whole gameplay within 3 android devices by utilizing BlueStacks 5 emulator, except the gameplay which is challenging between In-App User and In-Game player since this needs to have each other's contact numbers in their each App.
