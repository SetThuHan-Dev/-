Rope And Balls


A game replica of "RopeAndBalls" with different styles of effects and interactive objects.

I developed the whole game and approached its core technique with the combinations of 2D sprites (Balls) and 2D line renderer (rope) with 3D collision components.
I made this game for my previous company (Myanmar51 Studio) and also published to PlayStore and AppStoreConnect with the title of "RopeCupBall".
